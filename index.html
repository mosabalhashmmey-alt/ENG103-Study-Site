Chapter 1 - Introduction to Technical Communication

Click any beat to expand its content.

Technical communication is the process of sharing information that helps people understand technology, complete tasks, and solve problems effectively.

It focuses on clarity, accuracy, and usefulness.

  • User Manuals
  • Instructions
  • Reports
  • Emails
  • Technical Presentations
  • Business Documents

Technical writing focuses on helping the reader perform tasks or understand information quickly.

Academic writing focuses on research, analysis, and scholarly discussion.

  • Technical Writers
  • Engineers
  • IT Specialists
  • Project Managers
  • Researchers
  • Business Professionals
  • Clear
  • Accurate
  • Concise
  • Audience-Focused
  • Well Organized
  • Inform
  • Instruct
  • Persuade
  • Reports
  • Manuals
  • Memos
  • Proposals
  • Emails

Technical communication helps organizations improve efficiency, reduce errors, and communicate complex information clearly.
